When We Meet

Open Savannah regularly meets to discuss community ideas, work on civic tech projects and launch new projects into the wild. The fine folks at Bull St. Labs generously let us use their space to meet and hold events.

We hold two main types of regular events:

  • Open Civic Hacknights: - Held biweekly on the second and fourth Wednesday of each month at Bull Street Labs, Open Civic Hacknights act as a way for us to regularly brainstorm, collaborate, work on ongoing projects, get to know each other and establish a recurring, unstructured time to meet. These are more casual, informal meetups.
  • Community Idea Launchpads: - Held roughly once a month, these are larger, more community-wide evets that involve stakeholders from a wide sectors of disciplines who come together for a single evening for a 'mini-hacknight' to produce a civic app by the end of the event.
